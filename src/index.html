QuantumCoin

The quantum-resistant, Proof-of-Work cryptocurrency with a fixed 22,000,000 supply and a clear path from wrapped IOU to mainnet redemption.

What makes QuantumCoin different

What is QuantumCoin?

QuantumCoin (QC) is a Layer-1 blockchain focused on long-term durability: Proof-of-Work for simple, battle-tested consensus; post-quantum signatures to prepare for future threats; and a hard-capped supply of 22,000,000 coins issued on a predictable schedule.

Our design goal is “Bitcoin-style simplicity” where it matters (consensus, issuance) with careful upgrades where the future demands it (quantum resistance, safety controls).

Technology at a Glance

  • Consensus: Proof-of-Work (PoW). Simple and robust. No validator sets, no slashing.
  • Post-Quantum Signatures: Modern cryptography designed to remain secure as quantum hardware advances.
  • Lean Node Design: Full nodes, pruned nodes (low disk), and light/SPV modes for broader access.
  • Transparent Clients: Open source code, reproducible builds, and dependency audits planned.

Note: exact algorithms, parameters, and audits will be published as engineering milestones complete.

RevStop: User-Controlled Emergency Freeze

RevStop is an optional, owner-controlled safety switch for a single wallet in case of emergency (phishing, stolen device, exposed keys). It’s designed to reduce damage while you rotate keys.

What RevStop is:

  • Enabled and disabled only by the wallet owner (or their designated recovery key).
  • Temporarily prevents outgoing transfers from that wallet until the owner unfreezes.
  • Works without custodial seizure — no central party can take your funds.

What RevStop is not:

  • Not a global kill-switch. It affects only the wallet that opted in.
  • Not a bank freeze. There is no central authority moving or confiscating funds.
  • Not mandatory. Exchanges and advanced users can keep it off entirely.
Philosophy: security features should be voluntary, transparent, and local to the owner.

Tokenomics

  • Maximum Supply: 22,000,000 QC (hard cap).
  • Issuance: PoW mining over ~66 years with periodic halvings (Bitcoin-like schedule).
  • Premine/Dev Tax: None. All QC enters circulation through mining.
  • Distribution: Miners earn QC for securing the network. Open and permissionless.

The goal is credible scarcity and a transparent monetary schedule that’s easy to verify on-chain.

wQC — Wrapped QuantumCoin (IOU)

wQC is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum that represents future native QC on a 1:1 basis. It bootstraps community and liquidity while the Layer-1 completes.

Lifecycle (Mine-to-Redeem)

  1. Fixed cap: 100,000 wQC (no further minting).
  2. Trading: wQC can be listed on DEXs like Uniswap once liquidity is seeded.
  3. Mining the reserve: After mainnet, the team (and/or foundation) mines the first 100,000 QC under normal PoW rules — not pre-minted.
  4. Redemption: Holders redeem 1 wQC → 1 QC (unit-for-unit, not USD-pegged) during a public redemption window (e.g., 6–12 months).
  5. Sunset: After redemption closes, wQC is paused/retired so native QC remains the single live asset.
Price of wQC on DEXs is market-driven. Redemption promises units (QC), not dollars.

Contract address and Uniswap link will be published post-deployment.

Mining

QuantumCoin uses Proof-of-Work. Anyone can join by running a node and miner. To keep storage needs reasonable, nodes can run in pruned mode (keep only recent blocks) or as light clients on low-resource devices.

Node Profiles

  • Archival Node: Full history for explorers/research (larger disk footprint).
  • Pruned Node: Keeps recent blocks + full UTXO/state (~5–20 GB typical).
  • Light Client: Headers + proofs (hundreds of MB), great for mobile/desktop wallets.

Exact binaries, configs, and setup guides will be published ahead of testnet.

Roadmap

  1. Phase 1 — wQC IOU: Deploy ERC-20 (100k cap), seed initial DEX liquidity, publish transparent docs/dashboards.
  2. Phase 2 — Public Testnet: Multi-node network, explorer, wallets, RevStop opt-in testing.
  3. Phase 3 — Mainnet: Launch QC L1, begin mining the 100k redemption reserve under PoW.
  4. Phase 4 — Redemption Window: 1:1 wQC → QC, publish live ledger of burns/redemptions.
  5. Phase 5 — Sunset wQC: Retire the wrapped token; QC is the single live asset.

FAQ

Is QuantumCoin a fork?

No. The goal is an independent Layer-1 with Bitcoin-style economics and modern crypto primitives.

Is wQC backed by USD?

No. It’s an IOU for future units of QC. Market sets the USD price on DEXs.

How does RevStop differ from “admin freeze” tokens?

RevStop is opt-in and local to the wallet owner. There’s no central party that can seize or move funds.

How big will the chain get?

It depends on usage and parameters. Most users can run pruned nodes (~5–20 GB) or light clients (hundreds of MB).

When can I mine QC?

Mining guides and binaries will be released ahead of public testnet. Mainnet mining begins at launch.
